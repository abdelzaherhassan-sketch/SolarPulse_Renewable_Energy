# SolarPulse: AI-Driven Solar Energy Forecasting & Advanced Analytics Dashboard

An enterprise-grade, end-to-end data engineering and predictive modeling project developed for the National Graduation Project Showcase of the **Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative (DEPI)** under the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (MCIT), Egypt.

---

## 📌 Project Overview
**SolarPulse** is an advanced analytical platform designed to solve the intermittency problem of solar power generation. By integrating machine learning predictive models with interactive Business Intelligence (BI) dashboards, the system allows investors, operational managers, and green energy stakeholders to forecast solar energy output based on historical weather sensor data, thereby optimizing investment returns, technical efficiency, and grid integration.

### Core Objectives
* **Predictive Optimization:** Deploy a robust AI model to forecast daily/hourly solar power production ($kW/MW$) with high accuracy.
* **Data-Driven Decisions:** Transition raw, decentralized environmental logs into automated Data Pipelines.
* **Institutional Monitoring:** Provide real-time operational insights via an interactive Power BI dashboard for strategic monitoring of energy deployment.

## 📊 Business Metrics & Operational KPIs
The system monitors and optimizes critical operational and investment metrics:
* **Capacity Utilization Factor (CUF):** Evaluating the actual output compared to the maximum theoretical design capacity.
* **Mean Absolute Percentage Error (MAPE):** The core AI performance metric ensuring model forecasting reliability stays below acceptable institutional thresholds.
* **Investment Payback Period & ROI:** Integrated financial metrics within Power BI evaluating energy savings against installation capital expenditures (CAPEX).
